Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pix3lCover ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application.

1. Data Controller

The data controller for this application is Pix3lTools.

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us through our GitHub page or via X/Twitter.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally

Pix3lCover stores the following data in your browser's Local Storage:

  • Project data (images, text, settings)
  • Auto-save data
  • User preferences (e.g., dismissed warnings)

Important: This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not have access to your projects or images.

2.2 Analytics Data

We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. This may include:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on the application
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type
  • Country/region (approximate)

This data is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Type Purpose Duration
Local Storage Store your projects and preferences locally Until cleared by user
Vercel Analytics Anonymous usage statistics Session

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display text in your thumbnails. When you load the application, your browser makes requests to Google's servers to download font files. Google may collect:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser information

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 Vercel

Our application is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard web server logs. For more information, see Vercel's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Since your project data is stored locally on your device:

  • We do not retain any of your project data on our servers
  • You can delete your data at any time by clearing your browser's Local Storage
  • Data may be lost if you clear your browser data or switch devices

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can access your data directly in your browser's Local Storage
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete your data by clearing your browser's Local Storage
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export your projects as images at any time
  • Right to Object: You can disable analytics by using browser privacy features or ad blockers

7. Children's Privacy

Pix3lCover is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
